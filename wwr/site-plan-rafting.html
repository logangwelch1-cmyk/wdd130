Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the Dry Oar Rafting website is to be the primary online hub for booking white water rafting trips in the Southwest. We aim to inspire adventure, provide clear information on trips and safety, and convert visitors into customers through a seamless, mobile-friendly booking experience.

Audience

Our target audience is active individuals, couples, and families (primarily ages 25-55) with moderate disposable income looking for outdoor adventure vacations. They are often tech-savvy, use **mobile devices** or **laptops** for research, and require quick, well-organized details on trip difficulty, dates, and pricing to make a booking decision. They value safety, professionalism, and memorable experiences.
