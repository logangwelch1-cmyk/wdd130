Client Report

How did you decide what content and images to use for your site plan? I used AI to help generate some images and content ideas. Most of it was from the ideas I had beforehand. My dev suggested some extra images and it helped a lot.

How did you communicate with your developer? Email mostly although he wasn't in class often he was very responsive over email.

Was there any issues with communication? Not really, once we got going.

Did your developer meet your expectations for your site? Yes, I think more could have been done but for the time frame it was good.

What will you do differently next time you hire a team to do a project for you? I would do research on the devs beforehand to make sure they are a good fit.
